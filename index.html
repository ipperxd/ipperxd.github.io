About me

Welcome to my world of cutting-edge technology solutions and slightly exaggerated expertise. I'm Alex Cyberton, a self-proclaimed IT guru with an uncanny ability to solve problems I probably created in the first place.

For over 15 years (okay, maybe it’s closer to five, but who's counting?), I’ve been at the forefront of technological innovation. I’ve worked with global tech giants (wink) and single-handedly implemented multi-million-dollar solutions (cough Excel macros) that transformed businesses overnight—or so I told them.

From enterprise-level migrations to mysterious printer issues that defy logic, I tackle it all with a mix of wizardry and Googling skills. My clients appreciate my “solutions first, documentation later” approach—because who has time for details?

I pride myself on being available 24/7, though most of my real work happens after 2 AM when the coffee kicks in. And yes, I’ll always tell you your system needs upgrading, regardless of its actual state, because that’s how I roll (and pay my bills).

When I’m not saving the digital world, you can find me tweaking my LinkedIn profile, posting memes about IT struggles, and convincing clients that the cloud is just someone else’s computer—but don’t worry, I’ll make it sound way cooler.

Let’s connect, collaborate, and overpromise on what technology can do for your business today!
